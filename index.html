Who we are?

GoGreen-Decor is an Event decoration company inspired by nature. Our philosophy is based on Eco-design meaning all of our goods and services are environmentally responsible. Our dedicated team will make your objectives our own, creating unique experiences exceeding all expectation and the same time respecting the environment.

What we do?

We specialize in the design, production and management of both private and corpotate events in Ireland. We have an extensive list of hotels we work with, offering you different options according your preferences and budget.
