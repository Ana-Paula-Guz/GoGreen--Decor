Event Decoration Company inspired by nature

Who we are?

GoGreen-Decor is an Event decoration company inspired by nature. Our philosophy is based on Eco-design meaning all of our goods and services are environmentally responsible. Our dedicated team will make your objectives our own, creating unique experiences exceeding all expectation and the same time respecting the environment.

What we do?

We specialize in the design, production and management of both private and corpotate events in Ireland. We have an extensive list of hotels we work with, offering you different options according your preferences and budget.
The main aim of Eco-design is using fewer or better materials to reduce the environmental impact of products. That is why we choose recyclable or biodegradable, non-toxic, easily reproduce, renewable and low waste products making your event a unique experience feeling close to nature.

What we offer?

Our dedicated team has a vast experience in design, production and management of social and corporate events for over six years in Ireland. We take care all kind of events, from weddings or anniversaries till exhibitions or gala dinners. We create unique experiences with creativity, innovation and expertise.

  • Lighting
  • Marquees / Event structures
  • Ornamental tables and chair coverings
  • Candelabras
  • Majestic table centrepieces
  • Special lighting effects
  • Staging
  • Food and Beverage management (optional)
